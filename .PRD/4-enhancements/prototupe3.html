Aged, quiet gold. Used for backgrounds and large surfaces. The "whisper" of gold.

THE 5 GOLD COMMANDMENTS

  1. I. Gold is precious. Never exceed 7% of visual area.
  2. II. White is warm. Pure white (#FFFFFF) is forbidden.
  3. III. Darkness has depth. Use Void for canvas, Obsidian for surfaces.
  4. IV. Interaction must be earned. Hover states use the next gold tier.
  5. V. Clarity through hierarchy. Each color tier has one purpose.
