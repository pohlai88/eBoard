Key Philosophical Differences

GOLD CHARACTER

Your Theme: Aged, patinated brass (antique, historical)
My Theme: Polished, forged alloy (precise, intentional)

TYPOGRAPHY APPROACH

Your Theme: Editorial (Cormorant) + Forensic (JetBrains)
My Theme: Functional (Inter) + Forensic (JetBrains)

DARK BACKGROUNDS

Your Theme: Deeper (Void #0a0a0b), more gravitational
My Theme: Slightly lighter (#121212), more neutral canvas

LUXURY MANIFESTATION

Your Theme: Through restraint & material truth
My Theme: Through clarity & strategic hierarchy

INTERACTION PHILOSOPHY

Your Theme: Earned illumination, slow reveal
My Theme: Clear feedback, immediate recognition

WHITE SIMILARITY

Both use nearly identical warm off-whites (#f8f6f0 vs #F8F5F0), demonstrating shared understanding of reading comfort over pure white.
